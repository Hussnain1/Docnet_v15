Dr. Barliman Butterbur



Professional Statement

Dr. Butterbur is a board certified surgeon who is dedicated to providing unparalleled qualityto every patient. He has been practicing in the New York for more than 10 years. Dr. Butterburhas a particular interest in facial body contouring, cosmetic surgery and breast surgery.

Education

A native of Milwaukee, Barliman Butterbur, M.D. received his undergraduate degree from the NYUniversity and completed his medical degree at the University of Georgia. He trained under severalinternationally recognized plastic surgeons and perfected his surgical skills at the University of Kentucky.

Practice

  • Cosmetic Surgery
  • Breast Surgery
  • Body Contouring
  • Vascular Surgery
